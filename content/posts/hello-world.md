# Hello, World

Welcome to the blog. This post lives in `content/posts/` as Markdown and is
rendered on the server.

The site is **static by default**, with hydrated islands dropped in wherever a
page needs interactivity — demos, illustrations, and graphs.

- Server-rendered TSX with Hono
- Tailwind for styling
- Client islands for the interactive bits
